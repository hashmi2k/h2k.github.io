About me

Hi, I'm Ahsan Hashmi. A professional full-stack Web Developer with expert skills in Graphic Designing from Wah Cantt, Pakistan. If you want to know more about me, click the button below!




Personal life

I was born and raised in this city named Wah Cantt, Pakistan. I've spent my whole life here. Growing up was tough especially when you can't seem to find people who understand your mindset, but I've made it this far regardless of what I had to endure because the darkest nights make the brightest stars. As a kid, I was always into computers and latest technology, I started to learn about Graphic Design when I was 14 years old and I fell in love with creative design, this passion of learning more and more about computers led me to persue a career in the Computer Science field. Today I'm a professional Web Developer with a Bachelors Degree in Computer Science and I possess expert skills in Graphic Designing.


School & Education

I went to a private school named Jinnah Hall (2001 - 2011), after completing Matriculation, I completed my Intermediate education from BASE College (2011 - 2013). I did my Bachelors from COMSATS Institute of Information Technology in the field of Computer Science (2013 - 2017), I graduated in 2017.


Career

After graduating, I started looking for jobs and worked at a few firms but soon I realized that my passion was dying because of the continuous pressure from the firms I worked at. I quit my day job to become a full-time Freelancer because I wanted more than a tiring 9 to 6 routine. I officially started working as a freelance Graphic Designer and a Web Developer in 2018, before this I worked on projects that were outsourced to me. Graphic Designing is my passion and I'm a Web Developer by profession with 3 years of experience in the field. I have my own profile on Fiverr, Upwork and Freelancer.


Work & Projects

I've completed several Web projects in the last three years, aside from this, I've completed many Graphic designing projects and Motion Graphics projects using Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and After Effects, you can find the details of those projects in the 'Portfolio' section of my website.
